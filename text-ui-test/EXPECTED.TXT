Hey, welcome to my version of Duke and please make good use of it.
The bot is meant to be a bit cheeky at times so don't mind it.
Here is a list of commands you can try out:
?[todo ___] adds a todo task to your list.
?[deadline ___ /by ___] adds a deadline task to your list with a deadline.
?[event ___ /at ___] adds a event task to your list with a duration.
?[done (index of task)] marks a task as done in your list.
?[list] prints out your current list and status of tasks.
?[bye] exits the programme.
